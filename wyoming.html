Registration Information for Wyoming

Voter registration can be completed via mail, post office, or online. Physical voting locations are open from 7:00 am until 7:00 pm on election days.

If registered voters cannot vote on election days, they can do so within a 40 day "early voting" period in a county election office.



What else you should know

You cannot vote in Wyoming if you have been judged as "mentally incompetent" by a court, are a registered voter in another state, or are on parole for a felony.
