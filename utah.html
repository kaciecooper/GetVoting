Registration Information for Utah

You can register to vote or update your registration information:

• online

• by mail

• in person at your elections office

You must have resided in Utah at least 30 days prior to the election in which you intend to vote. Registered voters may vote at early voting locations only in certain counties.



What else you should know

Dates and times for early voting vary by location. Any registered voter may request an absentee ballot and vote by mail. Utah also offers permanent absentee voting status for any registered voter.
