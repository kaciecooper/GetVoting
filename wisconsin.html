Registration Information for Wisconsin

Voter registration can be completed via mail, post office, or online. Physical voting locations are open from 7:00 am until 8:00 pm on election days.

If registered voters cannot vote on election days, they can do so within a 2 week "early voting" period, stretching until the nearest Friday before each election. To register to vote, you must be a USA citizen, be 18 or older effectively, and reside within Wisconsin / live at least 10 days within the precint you are casting your vote in.



What else you should know

You cannot vote in Wisconsin if you are being jailed or on parole/probation for a felony.
