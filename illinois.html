Registration Information for Illinois

In Illinois you are able to register to vote via mail, online in person and on election day through your local election office. Polling places throughout the state are open from 6am to 7pm on election day. you are eligible to vote if you are a Illinois resident, a US citizen and 18 years old by the election day.

To register you must have your social security or driver's license/ID number. If you cannot provide one of those two, you may provide your Photo ID, Utility Bill, Bank Statement, Student ID, Government check or government issued document with your name and address.



What else you should know

You are able to vote in the primary election if you are 17, if you will be the age of 18 by the time of the general election.
