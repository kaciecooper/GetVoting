Registration Information for North Dakota

In North Dakota there is no voter registration. The only requirements to vote in North Dakota are as follows: Must be a U.S. citizen, a resident of the president you wish to vote in for at least 30 days, and be at least 18 years old.



What else you should know

In order to vote in North Dakota you are required to show ID or be able to sign an affidavit.
