Registration Information for Kansas

You must be a resident of the state and a person who wants to vote must be registered at least 21 days prior to the election along with being a resident during the time of registration.

If a citizen were to move from one county to another, their name would be removed from the voters list of their former residents and added to the county of the new resident.



What else you should know

You must be at least 18 years old and a US citizen
