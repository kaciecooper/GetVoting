Registration Information for North Carolina

In North Carolina you can vote by mail or in person at your local elections office. Polling hours are 6:30 a.m - 7:30 p.m. on Election Day. You can register to vote by mail or in person at your local elections office as well.

The requirements to vote in North Carolina are as follows: You must be a U.S. citizen, and a resident of North Carolina for at least 30 days in the precinct that you wish to vote.



What else you should know

If you are 17 years old, you can vote as long as you can provide proof that you will be 18 by the next general election.
