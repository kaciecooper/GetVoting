Registration Information for Idaho

In Idaho you can only vote by mail or by going in person on election day, you must also be a resident prior to 30 days of the election. To register to vote you must provide one of several documents such as social security number, drivers license number, photo ID, Proof of residency, or a government issued document with your name and address. To be able to vote in person you must show a valid ID such as: Federal issued ID, Driver's License, School ID, Tribal ID or US Passport.



What else you should know

you are not eligible to vote in Idaho if your are in Jail, Prison, or detention institute.
